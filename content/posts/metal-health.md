## Mental health

Expressing the reality of mental health as a teenager is incredibly difficult.
You’re expected to have it all figured out while your brain is still under construction.
Throwing a relationship into that mix? That’s a whole different level of complexity.

Honestly, trying to manage family expectations, a girlfriend’s needs, and a
social life all at once is a genuine art form. Add a full day of staring at
a screen, losing yourself in lines of code, and you’re running on empty before
the day is even half over.

The stress is real. Imagine the mental load: the "last-year" pressure,
the looming final exams, and the constant hum of a keyboard. It’s a lot to
carry, and it’s okay to admit that the balancing act is heavy.
